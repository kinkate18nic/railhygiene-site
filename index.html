The Reality of RailHygiene

RailHygiene is NOT an instant problem resolver. If you need immediate assistance during your journey (like a dirty toilet or missing amenities), you should use the official RailMadad app.

Instead, RailHygiene acts as a long-term accountability system. By consistently logging cleanliness scores for specific coaches on specific trains, we build a permanent, public reputation for the Indian Railways fleet.

Our Vision: Crowdsourced transparency that naturally incentivizes better train maintenance over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check train hygiene?

To check the historical cleanliness of your specific coach, you can enter your PNR into the RailHygiene site or search by Train Number to see past community ratings.

Are train coach ratings anonymous?

Yes, all train coach ratings submitted through RailHygiene are completely anonymous. We do not track or share your individual identity.
