RailMadad vs RailHygiene: Understanding the Difference

If you are travelling on the Indian Railways, maintaining cleanliness and hygiene is a top priority. Currently, two major applications exist to address this issue: RailMadad and RailHygiene.

While both aim to improve the passenger experience, they serve completely different purposes. Understanding the difference will help you know exactly which app to use at what time.

What is RailMadad?

RailMadad is the official grievance redressal portal created by the Ministry of Railways (Government of India). It is designed as an instant problem solver. If you are currently sitting on a train and notice that the toilet is dirty, the AC is not working, or you need medical assistance, you log a complaint on RailMadad to summon on-ground staff to your coach.

What is RailHygiene?

RailHygiene is an independent, community-driven rating platform. It acts as a long-term accountability system. Before you even book a ticket or board your train, you can use RailHygiene to check the historical cleanliness ratings of specific train coaches submitted by previous passengers. It works like TripAdvisor or IMDb, but for Indian train coaches.

Summary: Which should you choose?

Use RailMadad if you are on a train right now and urgently need someone to come clean your seat or fix a broken amenity.

Use RailHygiene to review your coach anonymously, establish a permanent public reputation for the train fleet, and check out what the cleanliness scores look like before your next trip.
